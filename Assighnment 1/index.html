Gamecocks Football

The Gamecocks are back in action!

The South Carolina Gamecocks will kick off their season this saturday, September second. They will face neighboring rivals North Carolina Blue Devils, a rematch of the Dukes Mayo Bowl from two sesons ago. The Gamecocks wil be looking to build off of the strong finish they had last season with the two big wins over Tennessee and Clemson. Head Coach Shane Beamer has been improving every year scince he took control of the program and the fans have big expectation for his third year.

The team will be lead by star quarterback Spencer Rattler, who will be looking to become more concistant in his second year at the helm.
